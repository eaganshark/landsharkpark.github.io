Welcome to Landshark Park!

Landshark Park - Establish 2015, the same year as American Pharaoh's Grand Slam Championship run. Horse racing at home is a great time saver. I don't lose precious time travelling to the local track to lose money in waging. My waging results are not any better at home, but it generally is more comfortable to enjoy a horse racing card.

A few general announcements:

1. I am not a professional handicapper. Views and/or predictions expressed on this site are my own and should not be copied.

2. I view horse racing as entertainment - any money wager is the "cost of adminission" which I do not expect any return on.

3. Any "results" on this site are not independently verified so I can make it look like I know how to predict winners in horse races. (I have a reputation to uphold.)

4. This site is intended for professional training purposes in JavaScript frontend frameworks like Angular, React and Vue.js.
